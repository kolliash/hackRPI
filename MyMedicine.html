Prednisone

When to use

Take 10 mg (2 tablets) at breakfast; Take 5 mg (1 tablet) at lunch; Take 5 mg (1 tablet) at dinner; Take 10 mg (2 tablets) at bedtime.

Importance

This is used when you have a flare-up of your symptoms or an asthma attack. This should be a short course to deal with symptoms

Warning

Take prednisone with breakfast so it does not upset your stomach. Taking prednisolone in the morning also means it's less likely to affect your sleep

Claritin

When to use

Claritin products are non-drowsy and can be taken at any time.

Importance

Claritin is used to treat sneezing, runny nose, watery eyes, hives, skin rash, hay fever, itching, and other cold or allergy symptoms

Warning

Claritin side effects are typically mild and can be managed at home.
